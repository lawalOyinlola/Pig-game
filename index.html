Lumina Dice Rules

Game Intensity

Select your difficulty level

Standard Protocol

Roll the dice to accumulate points. If you roll a 1, you lose your current points and pass the turn to the next player. Hold to add current points to your total.

Catch: You must reach exactly 100 points to win!

Hardcore Mode

Same as Normal, but if you roll a 1 OR exceed 100 on a hold, your points are DEDUCTED!

Minimum score is 0.

Turn Lost!

You rolled a 1. It is the next player's turn.
